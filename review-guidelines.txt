Before beginning to write your self
review think about:
• What specific projects did you work on
and outline your role in each
• Review the list of competencies
provided; these will appear on your self
review form
• Based on the brief descriptions of each
competency, provided your top 3
strengths as well as 3 areas of further
development
• What impact did you have on your
team; provide examples of how you
have contributed to your team activities
• Please note there is a character limit of
1,500 on the self review form
• Ensure your commentary is concise but
includes specific examples.
• Budget time to ensure you submit a
professional and well written self review
